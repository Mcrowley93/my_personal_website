About Me


Full Stack Software Development graduate with a keen eye for detail. Experienced background in the Security Industry has led to adept problem-solving skills, especially when handling when handling customer relations.

Having previously encountered obstacles that put me on a path parallel to the career-path i'd originally planned to follow, I took some time to reassess and think about what it was that I really enjoyed and could see myself forging a lifelong career from.
This was when I decided to look into Software Developement, as i've always had a passion for problem-solving and logical thinking, and the beauty of Software Development is that you are only limited by the depth of your knowledge, and your imagination.

So, having graduated from Code Institute's Full Stack Software Development course (focused in Website/Software Development - Front-end & Back-end) with a First Class Honours, I am bravely stepping out into the world of Software Development, and aim to work towards a Development role in the foreseeable future.
I specialise in HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and Python languages, using Django and Flask frameworks. I am passionate when it comes to creating a positive user experience, and always welcome a challenge.
